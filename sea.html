Welcome to S.E.A Aquarium

About

The S.E.A Aquarium is the 2nd largest aquarium in SouthEast Asia. It contains a total of 45,000,000 litres of water for more than 100,000 marine animals of over 800 species.The aquarium comprises 10 zones with 49 habitats. The centerpiece of the aquarium is the Open Ocean tank with more than 18,000,000 litres of water and 50,000 animals. Until 2014 when eclipsed by China's Chimelong Ocean Kingdom, it had the world's largest viewing panel, 36-metre wide and 8.3-metre tall, which is intended to give visitors the feeling of being on the ocean floor. They also have a conservation group called Guardians of the S.E.A.A., which supports research, education and public engagement efforts to protect the marine environment.
